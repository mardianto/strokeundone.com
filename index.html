No, the treatment is not painful at all. The most you should feel is a mild tingling sensation.

Generally, it is safe to say that the more time after the stroke, the more treatments are required. Daily treatments are twice a day, for a week: then, once a day; and, by the third week every other day. Treatments require 1-2 hours each visit.

You must provide us with a copy of your medical records and medications prior to treatment or agreement to treat. All medical information is held in the strictest confidence.

For example, fingers may begin to move or twitching may occur. We will not stop if we begin to get results.
